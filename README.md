##  Key Insights from Dashboard

- Total Sales: **522K+**  
- Consumer Segment contributes the highest revenue  
- Standard Class is the most used shipping mode  
- Technology and Office Supplies lead in sales  
- Phones, Chairs, and Binders are the top-selling sub-categories  
- COD and Online Payments are the most used payment modes
